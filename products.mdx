## Product Descriptions

### Pay by Bank

Pay by Bank is Ozow's core account-to-account solution. It lets your customers pay directly from their bank account to yours — no card, no cash, no delay. The payment flow is handled entirely on Ozow's hosted, bank-authenticated page, so your customers experience a familiar, trusted interface while you receive instant settlement confirmation.

[View Pay by Bank →](/products/pay-by-bank)

### Payouts

Ozow Payouts gives your business a simple, API-driven way to send funds to any South African bank account. Whether you're processing insurance claims, disbursing marketplace earnings, or running a loyalty rewards programme, Payouts handles the complexity of bank transfers so you don't have to.

[View Payouts →](/products/payouts)

### Refunds

Refunds removes the friction from the returns process. Instead of managing bank transfer details manually, you trigger a refund via API and Ozow handles the rest — routing funds back to the original payer's account quickly and reliably. Customers get their money back faster, and your support team spends less time on manual reconciliation.

[View Refunds →](/products/refunds)

### Ozow ME

Ozow ME is Ozow's peer-to-peer (P2P) payment solution, available as a native mobile app. It lets individuals and merchants send and receive money between accounts with confidence, and supports QR code payments for in-person transactions. Ozow ME is purpose-built for the mobile user, delivering a smooth, personalised experience on both iOS and Android.

[View Ozow ME →](/products/ozow-me)

### Pocket

Pocket is the digital wallet built into Ozow ME. Rather than relying on traditional EFT for every transaction, Pocket stores funds in a secure digital wallet that can be used for instant payments — making everyday purchases faster and safer than a standard bank transfer. Pocket furthers Ozow's mission of digitising cash for South African consumers.

[View Pocket →](/products/pocket)

## Recommended Integration Path

<Note>
  For all new integrations, Ozow recommends building against the **[OneAPI](/api/oneapi/overview)**. OneAPI consolidates Pay-In, Payouts, and Refunds into a single, unified API surface with consistent authentication, standardised response schemas, and long-term platform support. If you're starting a new project today, OneAPI is the integration path Ozow will continue to invest in and expand.
</Note>
