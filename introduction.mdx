Ozow is South Africa's leading account-to-account (EFT) payment platform, purpose-built to connect merchants and consumers across the country's banking ecosystem. Whether you're collecting payments on an eCommerce storefront, disbursing payouts to customers, issuing refunds, or enabling peer-to-peer money movement through Ozow ME, Ozow gives you a single, certified platform to handle it all — without touching card networks or storing sensitive payment credentials. With PCI DSS and ISO 27001 certifications, enterprise-grade 3-D Secure authentication, and reach across all 47 million South African bank account holders, Ozow is built for businesses that demand speed, security, and scale.

## Who This Hub Is For

Ozow Hub is the central documentation resource for **merchants** and **developers** integrating Ozow payment solutions into websites, mobile apps, and back-office systems. If you're evaluating Ozow's capabilities, setting up your first integration, or exploring advanced API flows, you'll find everything you need here — from quick-start guides to full API references.

## Why Ozow

- **Instant EFT, zero card dependency.** Payments clear account-to-account in real time, removing the cost and friction of card-network processing.
- **47 million bank account holders.** Ozow connects to every major South African bank, giving you the broadest possible reach from day one.
- **Enterprise-grade security.** Full PCI DSS compliance, ISO 27001 certification, and 3-D Secure authentication protect every transaction end-to-end.
- **Easy to integrate.** A clean REST API, ready-made plugins for popular eCommerce platforms, and dedicated support mean you can go from sandbox to live in days.
- **Full transaction lifecycle.** Accept payments, automate payouts, and process refunds through a single platform — no stitching together multiple providers.
