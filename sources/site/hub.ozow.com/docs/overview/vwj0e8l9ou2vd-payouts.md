# Payouts

**Ozow Payouts offers customers an easy way to request and withdraw funds from merchants in minutes. It's made possible by our easy to integrate API.**

## [Payouts as simple as that...](https://hub.ozow.com/#payouts-as-simple-as-that)

Ozow Payouts allows customers to request withdrawals from merchants. Thanks to Real-Time Clearing (RTC), those withdrawals clear in minutes. At the same time, payouts automation removes the manual payout process and associated staff and admin costs, allowing merchants to payout to unlimited recipients 24 hours a day, seven days a week, with one click of a button. All of this comes in a single, easy to integrate API.

Payouts automation eliminates the cumbersome and time consuming manual payout process, helping to reduce staff costs while increasing business efficiencies and customer satisfaction.

- Automated notifications are sent to the merchant as soon as an outcome is received from the bank
- Our API allows merchants to tailor the payout experience to their customers
- Merchants can utilise funds in their float for either Payout or Refunds
- Funds reflect in customer accounts within minutes of the request

## [Payout process](https://hub.ozow.com/#payout-process)

## [Account verification](https://hub.ozow.com/#undefined)

We provide CDV checks, a preliminary check on account validity. The combination of six-digit bank branch number and the bank account number are validated to ensure that they satisfy the check digit rules that each bank has. This does not check if the account itself is valid or active.

> Warning
> 
> ID verification is part of the process to authenticate your business is legitimate.

## [Payouts and reflection](https://hub.ozow.com/#payouts-and-reflection)

We offer real-time and standard transfers.

Real-time payouts are limited to R5 million per payout on weekdays in business hours, and R250 000 on weekends, or outside core business hours. Note that it can take up to 24 hours for merchants to receive confirmation of payout, even though the funds may have already reached the customer.

## [Testing](https://hub.ozow.com/#testing)

Merchants must test the required scenarios using the Mock API and Actual API in THE Staging environment. With the actual APIs in Staging, merchants can only test standard payouts. Payouts must be submitted before 4pm CAT to meet batch processing cut-off times. Payouts will be processed and completed by 9am the next day.
