## Get Up and Running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your Ozow merchant account">
    [Sign up at ozow.com/merchants](https://ozow.com/merchants) to get access to the Merchant Dashboard, where you'll find your API keys and site codes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Retrieve your credentials">
    Log in to the [Staging Dashboard](https://stagingdash.ozow.com) to get your test API Key, Private Key, and Site Code. Use these for all development and testing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Post your first payment request">
    Send a POST request to `https://stagingapi.ozow.com/PostPaymentRequest` with your site code, amount, and hash. See [Before You Begin](/integration/before-you-begin) for the full parameter list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Once testing is complete, swap to your production credentials from the [Live Dashboard](https://dash.ozow.com) and point your requests to `https://api.ozow.com`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
