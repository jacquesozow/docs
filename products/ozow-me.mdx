Ozow ME is a peer-to-peer payment solution designed for both individuals and businesses who need a fast, fee-free way to send and receive money. Whether you're splitting a restaurant bill with friends, collecting payment from a client, or running a small business that needs to request payment on the go, Ozow ME gives you multiple channels — QR codes, SMS, and WhatsApp — to initiate or receive a payment in seconds. Every cent goes directly into your linked bank account with no hidden costs, and real-time notifications confirm the moment a payment lands.

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="QR Code Payments" icon="qrcode">
    Generate your own personalised QR code tied to your Ozow ME ID. Share it with anyone — they scan it with their camera and the payment flow opens instantly. Compatible with both Ozow and Zapper QR codes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Get Paid via QR Code" icon="circle-dollar-to-slot">
    Display your personal QR code in person or digitally. The payer scans, enters their internet banking credentials, selects an account, and authorises — funds arrive in your bank account immediately.
  </Card>
  <Card title="SMS and WhatsApp Payment Links" icon="message">
    Send a payment request link directly to any South African cell number via SMS or WhatsApp. The recipient clicks the link and the Ozow payment flow opens on their device — no app download required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pay from an SMS Link" icon="mobile-screen">
    When you receive an SMS payment request, simply click the verified link. You are taken through the standard Ozow payment flow — select your bank, log in, select an account, and authorise. Done.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Ozow ME keeps you updated at every step. As soon as a payment is processed — whether incoming or outgoing — you receive a real-time notification so you always know the current status of your money.

## Zero Fees, Zero Hidden Costs

Ozow ME charges no transaction fees and no hidden costs for peer-to-peer payments. The full amount you send or request is the amount that moves — nothing is deducted in transit.

<Note>
  Withdrawals from your **Ozow Pocket** (the built-in digital wallet) do carry small fees depending on the withdrawal method. See the [Pocket](/products/pocket) page for the full fee schedule.
</Note>

## How to Link Your Bank Account

You link your bank account during the **Ozow ME sign-up flow**. The process walks you through connecting your South African internet-banking account so that all incoming payments land directly in your account and all outgoing payments are drawn from it.

<Info>
  Ozow ME works with all major South African banks that support internet banking. You can update or change your linked account from the profile settings inside the app at any time.
</Info>

## Example Use Case

<Card title="Splitting Dinner with Friends" icon="utensils">

## Compatible QR Standards

Ozow ME is compatible with both **Ozow QR codes** and **Zapper QR codes**, so you can accept payments from a wider base of users without requiring them to use a specific app.
