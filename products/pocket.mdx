Ozow Pocket is the digital wallet built directly into Ozow ME, and it takes Ozow's mission of digitising cash one step further. Instead of initiating a bank transfer every time you need to pay someone, you keep a balance in your Pocket that you can deploy instantly — no waiting for EFT clearing windows, no bank charges per transfer. Pocket-to-pocket payments are faster and more secure than standard EFT, use zero mobile data, and carry zero transaction fees for transfers between Pockets. You can top up your Pocket directly from your bank account, and withdraw your balance to cash or back to your bank whenever you need it.

## Registering for Pocket

Before you can use Pocket, you need to complete a short **Know Your Customer (KYC)** identity verification. Ozow's KYC process is built to be as quick and painless as possible.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the Registration Flow">
    Open Ozow ME and navigate to the Pocket section. Select **Register for Pocket** to begin.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the Live Selfie Verification">
    Take a **live selfie with a thumbs up** using your device's camera. This biometric step verifies your identity in real time and is required to activate your Pocket.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pocket Activated">
    Once your selfie is verified, your Pocket is activated and ready to use. Top it up and start making payments immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  The KYC process is a regulatory requirement. Ozow uses your live selfie solely for identity verification — it is not stored for any other purpose beyond compliance.
</Info>

## Pocket-to-Pocket Payments

Paying from your Pocket to another person's Pocket is instant and costs nothing. Follow these seven steps to complete a payment.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In to Your Pocket">
    Open Ozow ME and sign in to your Pocket with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Pay Now">
    On the Pocket home screen, tap **Pay Now** to start a new payment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Pay into Another Pocket">
    Choose **Pay into another pocket** from the payment options presented.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the Amount">
    Type in the exact amount you want to send.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the Recipient's Cell Number">
    Enter the South African cell number linked to the recipient's Pocket. Double-check the number before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the Payment">
    Review the payment summary — amount, recipient, and any reference details — and tap **Confirm**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate with OTP">
    Enter the one-time PIN (OTP) sent to your registered number to authorise the payment. Once validated, the funds move instantly to the recipient's Pocket.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Top Up Your Pocket

Keep your Pocket funded so you're always ready to pay. Topping up pulls funds from your linked bank account via the standard Ozow payment flow.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In to Your Pocket on Ozow ME">
    Open Ozow ME and sign in to your Pocket.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Balance Dropdown">
    Tap the **dropdown arrow** next to your displayed Pocket balance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Top Up">
    Choose **Top up** from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the Amount">
    Enter the amount you want to add to your Pocket.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the Payment Flow">
    Complete the Ozow payment flow — select your bank, log in, choose your account, and authorise. Your Pocket balance updates immediately once the payment is confirmed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Withdrawing from Your Pocket

You can withdraw your Pocket balance in three ways. Choose the method that best suits your needs — the process is the same for all three, with the key differences being the withdrawal fee and when you receive your funds.

### Withdrawal Methods and Fees

| Withdrawal Method | Fee per Transaction | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| EFT into bank account | R5.00 | Transferred to your linked bank account. Standard EFT clearing times apply. |
| Cash Express ATM | R10.00 | Available at every Cash Express branch nationwide. You receive a voucher via SMS. |
| Pick n Pay till point | R10.00 | Redeemable at any Pick n Pay store. You receive a voucher via SMS. |

### Withdrawal Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Balance Dropdown">
    Tap the **dropdown arrow** next to your Pocket balance and select **Withdraw**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Amount and Select Method">
    Enter the amount you want to withdraw, then select your preferred method: **EFT into my bank account**, **Cash Express ATM**, or **Pick n Pay till point**.

    Tap **Next** to continue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and Authorise">
    Review the withdrawal summary, which shows the amount, the applicable fee, and your account or voucher details. Tap **Withdraw**, then enter the OTP sent to your registered cell number to authorise.

    For Cash Express and Pick n Pay withdrawals, you will receive a **voucher number via SMS** and see it reflected in your transaction history. Vouchers carry an expiration date — redeem promptly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Cash vouchers (Cash Express and Pick n Pay) expire after a set period. If your voucher expires before you redeem it, contact Ozow support to arrange a re-issue.
</Warning>
